Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

We respect your right to control your personal data. This document provides clear instructions on how to request deletion of your data from Instagram Manager.

What Data Can Be Deleted?

1. Local App Data (Deleted by Us)

Data stored on our systems or within the Instagram Manager application:

  • Application settings and preferences
  • Authentication tokens
  • Any cached content
  • Analytics data
  • Support tickets and communication history

2. Instagram Data (Deleted by You)

Data stored on Instagram's servers and accessed via our API:

  • Your Instagram posts and media
  • Direct messages (DMs)
  • Comments and interactions
  • Profile information
  • Follower/following lists
Important: We do not store your Instagram data on our servers. All Instagram content is retrieved directly from Instagram's servers via the Meta Business API. To delete Instagram data, you must use Instagram's own tools and settings.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

Send a deletion request to:

Email: support@instagram-manager.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request - [Your Username]

Include in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Instagram username associated with the app
  • The email address used to register
  • Specific data types you want deleted (or request deletion of all data)
  • Confirmation that you own the account

Option 2: GitHub Issue

Submit a data deletion request via our public repository:

URL: https://github.com/zainhthegreat-bot/instagram-manager/issues

Issue Title: Data Deletion Request - [Your Username]

Required Information:

  • Your Instagram username
  • Specific data to be deleted (or all data)
  • Contact email for verification
  • Proof of account ownership (we may request additional verification)

Deletion Process

Step 1: Verification

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  1. Send you a confirmation email within 3 business days
  2. Request additional verification if needed
  3. Confirm your identity as the account owner

Step 2: Deletion

Once verified, we will:

  • Delete all local app data associated with your account within 30 days
  • Provide confirmation when deletion is complete
  • Retain only basic logs required for legal compliance (anonymous, no personal data)

Step 3: Instagram Data (Your Responsibility)

We cannot delete data stored on Instagram's servers. To remove Instagram data:

Delete Instagram Posts and Media:

  1. Open Instagram app or website
  2. Go to your profile
  3. Select the post you want to delete
  4. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) → "Delete"

Delete Direct Messages:

  1. Open Instagram Direct
  2. Select the conversation
  3. Tap the user's name at the top
  4. Scroll down → "Delete Chat" or "Unsend Messages"

Delete Comments:

  1. Navigate to the post with your comment
  2. Tap your comment
  3. Select "Delete Comment"

Delete Your Entire Instagram Account:

If you want to permanently delete your Instagram account:

  • Visit: https://www.instagram.com/accounts/remove/request/permanent/
  • Follow Instagram's deletion process
  • Note: This cannot be undone

Timeline for Deletion

Data Type Deleted By Timeframe
Local app data Instagram Manager Within 30 days of verified request
Authentication tokens Instagram Manager Within 7 days of verified request
Instagram posts User Immediate (via Instagram app/website)
Direct messages User Immediate (via Instagram app/website)
Comments User Immediate (via Instagram app/website)
Instagram account User Up to 30 days (via Instagram's process)

What Happens After Deletion?

Local App Data:

  • Permanently removed from our systems
  • Cannot be recovered
  • You will need to re-authenticate if you use the app again

Instagram Data:

  • Only you can delete Instagram data
  • Data deleted from Instagram will no longer appear in our app
  • Some cached data may appear briefly until refreshed

Exceptions: Data We Cannot Delete

We may retain certain data in the following circumstances:

  1. Legal Requirements: Data required by law, court order, or government request
  2. Security Logs: Anonymous security logs for preventing fraud and abuse
  3. Account Recovery: Minimal data needed for account recovery (for 90 days after deletion)
  4. Ongoing Transactions: Data related to incomplete transactions or support requests

Revoke App Access

If you want to stop the app from accessing your Instagram data immediately:

Method 1: Via Instagram App

  1. Go to Settings → Security → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Instagram Manager" in the list
  3. Tap it and select "Remove Access"

Method 2: Via Meta Business Suite

  1. Go to https://business.facebook.com/settings
  2. Navigate to Business Integrations
  3. Find and remove Instagram Manager

This will immediately revoke our app's access to your Instagram data.

Right to Data Portability

Before requesting deletion, you may request a copy of your data:

  • Submit a request via email or GitHub issue
  • Specify "Data Export Request" in the subject line
  • We will provide your data in a readable format within 30 days

Questions or Concerns?

If you have questions about data deletion or these instructions:

  • Email: support@instagram-manager.com
  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/zainhthegreat-bot/instagram-manager/issues
  • Privacy Policy: Read our full Privacy Policy

Your data privacy is important to us. We are committed to processing deletion requests promptly and transparently.
