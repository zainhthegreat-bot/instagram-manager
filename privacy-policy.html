Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Instagram Manager ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Instagram Data

With your explicit consent and authorization, the App may access the following data from your Instagram account(s):

  • Posts: Content, captions, hashtags, and engagement metrics
  • Direct Messages (DMs): Conversation history and message content
  • Comments: Comments on your posts and comments you've made
  • Profile Information: Username, bio, profile picture, and account settings

2.2 Meta Business API Data

The App uses the Meta Business API to securely access your Instagram data. We only request the permissions necessary for the App to function properly.

2.3 Local Data Storage

All data collected by the App is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Cached Instagram posts and comments
  • Message history
  • Application settings and preferences
  • Authentication tokens (stored securely on your device)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of managing Instagram accounts
  • To display and organize your Instagram content within the App
  • To enable features such as post scheduling and comment management
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support when requested

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

4.1 Required by Law

We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests from government authorities.

4.2 Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party service providers to:

  • Provide technical infrastructure and support
  • Conduct data analysis
  • Improve the App's features

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your Instagram data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data once it's downloaded to your device.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage of authentication credentials using your device's keychain/keystore
  • Regular security updates and patches

5.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your device secure with a password, PIN, or biometric lock
  • Not sharing your device with unauthorized persons
  • Logging out of the App when using a shared device

6. Meta Business API and Data Access

The App uses the Meta Business API, which operates under Meta's data use policies. By using this App, you agree to Meta's terms and policies regarding your Instagram data.

For more information, visit:

  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Terms of Use

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

7.1 Access

You can view all data stored by the App within the application interface.

7.2 Deletion

You can delete your data from the App at any time through the application settings. Deleting data from the App does not delete it from Instagram's servers.

7.3 Export

You may export your local data from the App in standard formats.

7.4 Revoke Access

You can revoke the App's access to your Instagram account at any time through Instagram's settings or Meta's developer tools.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services. You can delete your data at any time through the App's settings.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our GitHub repository and within the App.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Contact us through our official channels

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to transparency and giving you control over your data.
